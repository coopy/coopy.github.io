Per Nilsson | Software and Interaction Design

Hi, my name is Per. I'm a software engineer, interaction designer, technologist, skateboarder, free runner, traveler and life artist.

I lead a talented team of JavaScript developers at Formidable Labs in Seattle.

I studied interaction design under David Cuartielles, inventor and cofounder of the Arduino open-source platform for physical interaction. I later went on to teach an introductory Arduino course at Simon Fraser University in Vancouver, BC and at 911 Media Arts Center in Seattle, WA.
